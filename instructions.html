Instructions

These are the steps you take to play this game!

Step 1: If you are new to this game, click sign up. If you already have an account, you can click log in.

Step 2: Once you have logged in/signed up, you can click on one of the categories.

Step 3: Answer the questions! If you get the questions right, you get points and those points can get you hints!

Step 4: Play the fun game at the end! Once you are done, you can exit or do new categories.
